Support

Need help with ChannelCove or want to report a channel issue? Send a short note and we will review it.

Contact

Helpful Details

  • Your device model and iOS version
  • The channel name

Channel Availability

ChannelCove uses selected official public sources and embedded players. Some live streams may change, become temporarily unavailable, display third-party errors, or be restricted by source, region, network, or device conditions.

Optional Support Tips

Tips are optional App Store in-app purchases. They help support testing, App Store costs, and channel maintenance, but they do not unlock channels, remove ads, or change playback access.
