Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 28, 2026

Overview

ChannelCove is published by ELYXAN LLC. The app is designed to provide a simple way to browse selected official live news and science/nature channels while collecting as little information as practical.

Information We Collect

ChannelCove does not require an account. The app may store local preferences on your device, such as favorite channels, last watched channel, and playback preferences. These preferences stay on your device unless a future version clearly explains otherwise.

Embedded Video Players

ChannelCove uses official embedded video players and official source pages from third-party services, including YouTube and selected channel providers. Those services may process data according to their own privacy policies when you load or interact with their players, including playback, device, network, and advertising-related information.

Location

ChannelCove does not use your location for its own account, analytics, or advertising system. Some third-party players or source pages may request location access to determine whether a stream is available in your area. If that happens, the request is controlled by the third-party source or platform.

Feedback Email

If you contact us by email, we will receive the information you choose to send, such as your email address, message content, and any device details you include. Please do not send sensitive personal information.

In-App Purchases

Optional support tips are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive your full payment card details. Tips do not unlock channels or change playback access.

Children

ChannelCove is not directed to children and is not submitted as a Kids Category app. The app does not provide accounts, profiles, comments, chat, or user-submitted content.

Changes

We may update this policy as ChannelCove changes. The latest version will be posted on this page.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact channelcove@elyxan.com.
